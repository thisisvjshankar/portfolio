I'm Vijay — a Senior Business Analyst with over 7 years of experience working at the intersection of business strategy and technology delivery in global financial services. I've had the privilege of working with world-class organizations like Citigroup, Bank of America, HSBC, and Westpac, helping them navigate complex regulatory landscapes, implement enterprise systems, and build products that users actually love.

My superpower is turning ambiguity into clarity. Whether it's decomposing a complex regulatory requirement, facilitating discovery sessions with a roomful of stakeholders who disagree, or writing user stories that developers can actually act on — I make the messy business of defining "what should we build" feel structured and manageable.

I'm certified as a CBAP, Agile Analysis Certification holder, Scrum Master, and Product Owner — not because certifications define me, but because I believe in the craft of business analysis and I invest in getting better at it. I'm now based in Calgary, looking for my next challenge where I can help a great team ship great products.

Case Study 01 · Citigroup

Impact Analysis Application

Infosys via Citigroup · 2022–Present
🔍 The Problem
Citi had 150+ downstream systems dependent on upstream data feeds. Any change to upstream systems could silently break downstream applications — with no visibility into what would be affected.
⚙️ My Approach
Led full requirements gathering and product management for a new internal tool. Mapped all upstream-downstream dependencies, defined the data model, facilitated design workshops with multiple business SMEs, and authored the BRD and user stories for the development team.
✅ Outcome
150+ downstream application teams now have full visibility into change impacts before releases. Reduced post-release surprises and enabled faster, safer deployments across Citi Tech.
Case Study 02 · HSBC Bank

Oracle Fusion Financials — Testing Automation

Infosys via HSBC · 2019–2021
🔍 The Problem
Every quarterly upgrade and country rollout for Oracle Fusion Financials required a full manual regression test cycle — consuming enormous time and FTE capacity, with inconsistent coverage.
⚙️ My Approach
Led the automation initiative using Tricentis TOSCA. Designed a reusable regression pack covering Procurement, Expenses, and Fixed Assets modules. Defined test scope, coordinated the automation team, and presented the business case to senior stakeholders.
✅ Outcome
Saved 3 full-time testing efforts per cycle. Reduced manual testing by 45% and enabled the regression suite to be reused across all future upgrades and country rollouts.
Case Study 03 · Bank of America

SEC Regulatory Compliance — Books & Records

Infosys via Bank of America · 2017–2019
🔍 The Problem
BofA's Global Markets division faced gaps in regulatory compliance for Books & Records management under SEC 17a-3, 17a-4, MSRB, and Volcker — with inconsistent data quality across multiple applications.
⚙️ My Approach
Collaborated across Front Office, Legal, Finance, Operations, and Tech Risk to gather requirements and design a data quality strategy. Conducted compliance audits, gap analyses, and produced comprehensive regulatory documentation.
✅ Outcome
Achieved a 35% compliance improvement across targeted regulations. Established standardized data retention and purging protocols now used across multiple Global Markets applications.
